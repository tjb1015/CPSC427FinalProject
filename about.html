About the SVG drawing package


This is a client-side drawing package including tools for drawing in the web browser


The objects


Rectange, Triangle, Pentagon, Octagon, Arrow
To draw:
  1. In the menu system, click 'Shapes'.
  2. Pick your desired shape with in the sub menu.
  3. Then proceed to choose a starting point on the screen to draw.
  4. Click on the mouse and drag. Where you release will be the ending point of the shape.
  5. If you wish to resize the shape, simply click on the shape which will show the control points and drag the control points to a desired position.
  6. If you want to move the shape, click on it and drag to desired position and let go of the mouse.
Ngon
To draw:
  1. In the menu system, click 'Shapes'.
  2. Pick ngon with in the sub menu.
  3. Choose a point on the screen and click.
  4. Continue to choose points on the screen until you decide you have enough points.
  5. To finish the shape, make your last click on your original starting point.
  6. If you wish to resize the shape, simply click on the ngon and control points will appear. Then proceed to click on and drag the control points.
  7. If you want to move the ngon, click on it and drag it to the desired location.
Ellipse and Circle
To draw:
  1. In the menu system, click 'Shapes'.
  2. Pick either circle or ellipse with in the sub menu.
  3. Choose a point on the screen and click, proceed to drag to get desired size.
  4. If you want to move the circle or ellipse, click on it and drag it to the desired location.
Line
To draw:
  1. In the menu system, click 'Shapes'.
  2. Pick the line option with in the sub menu.
  3. Choose a point on the screen and click, drag and let go to end the line.
  4. If you want to move , click on it and drag it to the desired location.
  5. If you want to resize the line, click on it and control points will appear. Then click on the control point and drag to desired location.
Bezier Curve
To draw:
  1. In the menu system, click 'Shapes'.
  2. Pick the bezier curve option with in the sub menu.
  3. Click at the position of the first (and final point).
  4. Drag the mouse to draw a straight line.
  5. Click (and then drag) at each position where you want a new point to appear. The slope and length of the resulting line, controls the direction the curve will head.
  6. Click on the starting point once more to terminate the drawing; dragging will adjust the slope of the final segment of the curve.
Draw
To draw:
  1. In the menu system, click 'Brush'.
  2. Pick either brush 1, brush 2, or brush 3 for desired thickness.
  3. Hold the mouse down and have fun drawing on the screen.

Editing

Copy, Delete, Flip, Pick Color, Re Color, Unselect, View Source
To edit:
  1. Click on the shape.
  2. When you click on the shape the edit menu will appear.
  3. Choose what ever editing function you would like.
  • Copy - Makes a copy of the shape you have selected.
  • Delete - Deletes the shape you have selected
  • Flip - Flips the shape 180 degrees.
  • Pick Color - Allows you to you use the color picker (see directions) to change the color to your desired choice.
  • Re Color - Changes the color of the shape you have selected randomly.
  • Unselect - Unselects the shape and removes the control points.
  • View Source - Allows you to view the source code of the shape you have selected.
Color Picker

To use:
  1. Pick Hue, Saturation, or lighting.
  2. Slide the rectangles to desired color.

The Grid

Grid
To use as a measurement guide:
  1. In the menu system, click 'Grid'.
  2. To turn the grid on simply choose the yes option.
  3. To turn the grid off simply choose the no option.
  4. The grid is uses a 100 x 100 scale.
Note: Only works properly in Chrome. In IE and Opera the grid size is infinity, which is strange. Doesnt work at all in Firefox.

Clear svg area

New
To clear the svg area:
  1. In the menu system, click 'File'.
  2. In the sub menu choose new.
  3. This clears all of the shapes and editing the user has done with in the svg drawing area.
  4. Do not use this unless you are sure you would like to start over.
